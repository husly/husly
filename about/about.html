ABOUT US!

Welcome to Husly! (Husly means shelter in Danish) We provide information about animals!
We created this page for the population of the Bay Area that want to own or already own animals!
It's a problem how people find animals, but they don't know what to do or how to care of it.
In order to fix this problem, we have information about where animal shelters and shops are.
So when you find an animal in need, you can find your local shelter and bring it there.
Don't know how to take care of your pet? We can a care tab that tells you the basics of how to take care of your animal!
If you still need help information about adopting, visit the Q&A tab for frequently asked questions about finding/adopting animals!
