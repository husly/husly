Cat Care

Food

  • Foods with taurine
  • Canned cat food
  • Dry cat food
  • Water at all times
  • Treats only 5-10% of diet

Feeding

  • 8 weeks-6 months: 3 times a day
  • 6 months-1 year: 2 times a day
  • 1 year+: 1 time a day

Daily

  1. Brush coat (only bath a few times in a year)
  2. Wear collar and ID tag for outside
  3. Scratching (cut nails every 2-3 weeks)
  4. Clean litter box

Health

  • See vet once a year
  • Female cats spayed by 5 mosnths
  • Male cats neutered by 5 months

Supplies Checklist

  • Cat food
  • Food & water dish
  • Toys
  • Brush & comb
  • Collar & leash
  • Scratching post
  • Litter box
  • Cat bed/box
