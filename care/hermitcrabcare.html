HERMIT CRAB CARE!

1. Make sure you have a tank big enough
for your hermit crab to move around!
2.Make sure you have the right
humidity (around 70%-80%) & the right temperature
(around 75-85)because they are tropical! :)
3. Fill your crabs tank up with sugar-sized
sand or compressed coconut fiber, make sure it is high enough so your crab can bury itself!
4. Get your crab extra shells because
they love to change once they get too big
for their current one!
5.Crabs love to climb! Get your crabs some
climbing toys that they could use.
6. Have a steady diet for your crabs! They can eat seafood
and other raw meat like chicken and beef. Aside from protein they love fresh fruit that you carry in your household.
Donâ€™t forget to clean up the leftover food in the morning!
7.Hermit crabs can be very friendly! Be gentle with your crab,
handle it with care, though they pinch when they feel attacked,
give them some time to feel unthreatened.
8. Do not give your crab tap water! Tap water will slowly kill
them due to the chlorine. You can use betta-safe solution
to make your water safe for your crab, also be
sure to provide them with some saltwater so
they can choose between their water choices!
