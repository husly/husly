DOG CARE


What they eat!

Adult Dogs:

  • Dry food (can be mixed with water,
    broth or canned food)
  • Water at all times
  • Less than 10% of daily food
    • Cottage cheese
    • Cooked egg
    • Fruits and vegetables

Puppies:

  • Puppy food
  • Water at all times

How to feed them!

  • 8-12 weeks: 4 meals a day
  • 3-6 months: 3 meals a day
  • 6 months to 1 year: 2 meals a day
  • 1 year+: 1 meal a day

Daily needs

  1. Excercise
    (throw ball, run)
  2. Brush coat
    (only bath a few times in a year)

Pay attention to their health

  • See vet once a year
  • Flea comb to find and remove fleas
  • Female dogs should be spayed by 6 months
  • Male dogs neutered by 6 months

Everyday Supplies Checklist!

  • Dog food and treats
  • Food & water dish
  • Toys (safe-chew)
  • Brush & comb
  • Collar & leash
  • Dog bed/box
  • Dog toothbrush
