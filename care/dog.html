Dog Care

Food

Adult Dogs:

  • Dry food (can be mixed with water, broth or canned food)
  • Water at all times
  • Less than 10% of daily food
    • Cottage cheese
    • Cooked egg
    • Fruits and vegetables

Puppies:

  • Puppy food
  • Water at all times

Feeding

  • 8-12 weeks: 4 meals a day
  • 3-6 months: 3 meals a day
  • 6 months to 1 year: 2 meals a day
  • 1 year+: 1 meal a day

Daily

  1. Excercise (throw ball, run)
  2. Brush coat (only bath a few times in a year)
  3. If dog needs to be carried:
    • Puppy: Place one hand under chest and one hand to support the hind legs and rump
    • Adult: Lift from under while supporting the chest with one arm and the rear with the other

Health

  • See vet once a year
  • Flea comb to find and remove fleas
  • Female dogs should be spayed by 6 months
  • Male dogs neutered by 6 months

Supplies Checklist

  • Dog food and treats
  • Food & water dish
  • Toys (safe-chew)
  • Brush & comb
  • Collar & leash
  • Dog bed/box
  • Dog toothbrush
